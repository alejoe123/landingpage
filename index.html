Jeff Bezos

Born on October 10, 1944

Jeff Bezos, born on January 12, 1964, is an American entrepreneur and the founder of Amazon.com, the world's largest online retailer.Bezos's entrepreneurial spirit didn't stop at Amazon. He ventured into various industries, including cloud computing with Amazon Web Services (AWS), which has become a leading player in the field. Throughout his career, Bezos has received numerous accolades for his contributions to technology and business, including being named Time magazine's Person of the Year in 1999 and 2020. As of the knowledge cutoff in September 2021, Bezos stepped down as Amazon's CEO, allowing him to focus on other ventures, such as his space exploration company, Blue Origin. With his remarkable achievements and unparalleled vision, Jeff Bezos has left an indelible mark on the business world, inspiring aspiring entrepreneurs and shaping the future of e-commerce and beyond.
